Construction

Executing all the premiere phases of construction management and delivering the project successfully to the end users. Projects belonging to Industrial, Power, Oil & Gas, Commercial and Residential facilities are being executed to the satisfaction of our customers. Services include:

Engineering

We are tied up with global engineering firms which can perform the Project studies e.g. Feasibility studies, Noise level, Hazardous area classification, Earth resistance and Soil Resistivity, Piping Stress analysis, Illumination (Lux Level) studies and other significant analysis required before and during executing the Projects. Detailed Engineering can be done and engineering drawings can be developed as per the required Project Specifications. As-Builting Services are available to develop the Final Drawings incorporating the Red-lined changes done at site during project stages.

Procurement

Based on the engineering specifications provided or the Technical parameters generated during the detailed engineering, procurement services can be provided which include the In-Kingdom (IK) or Local Procurement as well as Out of Kingdom (OOK) Overseas procurement inclusive of the customs clearance and Freight Forwarding services.

Construction (Project Controls, Quality Management, HSE)

Subcontracts related to Civil, Mechanical, Electrical, Piping and Instrumentation works can be made with the valued clients and the associated construction works can be executed. The contracts can be Lump sum, EPC or Reimbursable Man-hours based. The construction is done incorporating all the Project Management requirements including Planning, Reporting, Auditing, Project Controls (including Cost control, Progress measurement, monitoring of schedule and Quantity analysis) Quality and safety Management.

Commissioning & Turnover

Precommissioning and Commissioning services including supply of consumables, calibration of equipments and instruments, Piping tests including Hydro testing, loop checking, Testing of Electrical and Instrumentation panels, Transformer Oil Filtration and testing is being done at reasonable cost and within genuine time. Commissioning Dossiers can be documented as per the project requirements. Turnover documentation can be designed as well as prepared.
