Project Execution
Electrical, Mechanical, Civil, Piping, Instrumentation, Hydraulic

Contracts related to the Electrical, Mechanical, Civil, Piping, Instrumentation and Hydraulic works are taken and executed vide specific type of contracts e.g. EPC or vide subcontracts. The construction is done incorporating all the Project Management requirements including Planning, Reporting, Auditing, Project Controls (including Cost control, Progress measurement, monitoring of schedule and Quantity analysis) Quality and safety Management.

General Services
Power, Sanitation, Cleaning, Potable Water Services

Contracts related to General Services works in Ongoing Construction projects as well as Commissioned or Operating Plants are executed on annually renewable basis. The facilities provided can be Supply of Electrical Power, Sanitation, Catering, Cleaning and supply of Potable Water.
